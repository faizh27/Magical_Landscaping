Our Story & Mission

Creating beautiful outdoor spaces with passion and expertise since 2010

Our Humble Beginnings

Magical Landscaping was founded in 2010 by John and Sarah Greenfield with a simple vision: to transform ordinary outdoor spaces into extraordinary living environments.

What started as a small family business with just two employees has grown into one of the region's most trusted landscaping companies, serving hundreds of satisfied clients.
